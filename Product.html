Personal Loans

Quick access to funds for personal needs

Our personal loans are designed to help you meet immediate financial needs, whether it's for medical expenses, education, home improvements, or other personal emergencies.

Key Features

  • No collateral required for loans up to UGX 5M
  • Flexible repayment terms
  • Quick approval process
  • Competitive interest rates
  • No hidden fees
  • Early repayment options

Eligibility Criteria

  • Ugandan citizen aged 21-65 years
  • Regular monthly income (employed or self-employed)
